Overview of Our Services

With over 15 years of experience, our team specializes in software, FPGA, and electronic hardware development, focusing on efficiency and performance for mission-critical applications.

We have a highly skilled team with expertise in Software (C, C++), FPGA (Verilog, VHDL), and Electronic Hardware development. We primarily work on embedded systems with microcontrollers such as ESP32, STM32, Nordic nRF Series, and Arduino, delivering efficient and reliable solutions tailored to specific requirements.

We work on both contract and project-based engagements, prioritizing "Efficiency First Design" principles to optimize performance, power consumption, and resource utilization for all our clients.

Embedded Systems

We specialize in developing sophisticated embedded systems using a variety of microcontroller platforms.

Our team has extensive experience working with popular microcontroller platforms including ESP32, STM32, Nordic nRF Series, and Arduino. We develop customized firmware and software solutions that effectively leverage the capabilities of these platforms.

We also have expertise in Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS), Bare Metal CPU programming, and Yocto/Petalinux-based Embedded Linux. Our team can modify Linux Device Trees and customize Embedded Linux distributions to meet specific requirements.

Communication Protocols

We implement a wide range of communication protocols for diverse connectivity needs.

Our team has implemented numerous communication protocols on both FPGA and CPU platforms. These include BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy), WiFi 6, I2C, SPI, UART, LoRa, TCP/IP, UDP, and RS-232, among others.

We also have experience developing custom protocols for specialized applications. Our expertise extends to implementing advanced protocols such as SFP+, 1-wire, and PCI-e, ensuring seamless integration and communication between different system components.

FPGA Development

We offer comprehensive FPGA development services using Verilog and VHDL.

Our team has proven experience working with various Xilinx FPGA chips, including Kintex, Virtex, ZYNQ, ZYNQ Ultrascale, and Spartan families. We develop custom IP cores and implement complex digital logic designs to meet specific performance and functionality requirements.

We are confident in our ability to handle board bring-ups and have successfully implemented numerous communication protocols on FPGA platforms. Our expertise enables us to create highly efficient, high-performance solutions for challenging applications.
