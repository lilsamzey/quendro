Our Expertise

We specialize in embedded systems development across a wide range of technologies and platforms

Rapid Prototyping

We offer rapid prototyping embedded solutions to help you bring your ideas to life quickly and effectively.

Embedded C/C++ Development

We offer professional Embedded C/C++ development for your projects. Whether you are starting a project from scratch, continuing an existing project, or adding new features, we are here for you at every stage.

Microcontroller Development

We develop projects with popular microcontrollers such as ESP32, STM32, Nordic nRF Series and Arduino.

Communication Protocols

We use various communication protocols such as BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy), WiFi 6, I2C, SPI, UART, LoRa, TCP/IP, UDP and RS-232.

Sensor Data Processing

We process data from sensors such as pO2 sensor, CO2 sensor, flow sensor, load cell, hall sensor and accelerometers.

Actuator Control

We control actuators, such as motors, pumps and valves, using data gathered from sensors.

Operating Systems

Our team has extensive experience in "bare metal" programming, embedded Linux as Yocto, Petalinux and RTOS platforms, including FreeRTOS and Zephyr RTOS.

FPGA Development

We have proven experiences on different Xilinx Families (Kintex, Virtex, ZYNQ, ZYNQ Ultrascale, Spartan).
